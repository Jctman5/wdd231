Undertow - Site Plan

Site Name

Undertow

The name "Undertow" represents the emotional pull and depth of the band's shoegaze/grunge sound—something that drags listeners in beneath the surface. It reflects both the heavy, distorted elements and atmospheric layers of the music.

Optional domain: undertowband.com

Site Purpose

The website serves as a central hub for the band Undertow, providing information about music releases, upcoming shows, band members, and media. It also allows fans to connect with the band and stay updated on new content.

Scenarios

  • Where can I listen to Undertow’s latest songs or albums?
  • When and where is Undertow performing next?
  • How can I follow or contact the band?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Black (#0d0d0d) – Background
  • Purple (#8a7cff) – Headings
  • Gray (#e0e0e0) – Body text
  • Light Blue (#488ef8) – Accents

Typography

  • Courier New – Headings (grunge feel)
  • Arial – Body text (readability)
